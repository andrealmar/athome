The At Home Difference

We’re not like the others.
At Home Services is unique in the home repair industry. We help local technicians build their businesses, allowing them to focus on the most important thing: satisfaction and happiness in your own home.

Personalized service.
Our goal is to provide a hero for your home, and that’s not just a gimmick. When you have a repair situation arise, we want it to be the same technician who comes to your home as the time before. They’ll know your name and how best to help you with access to a database, calendar, parts lists, and much more. What’s more, you’ll know them.

Truly a Home Hero you can trust.
Your neighborhood’s Hero is their own business owner. Your satisfaction is their responisbility and ultimate goal. If you have questions or concerns, your Hero wants to know about them. Estimates, payments, and timelines on projects will all be worked out between your Hero and you.
